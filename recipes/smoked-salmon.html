Spice Smoked Salmon

Description

Grilled salmon seasoned with a smokey rub served with a fresh black beans and corn salsa.

These grilled salmon are steaks rubbed with a spicy blend of smoked paprika, chipotle chili powder, brown sugar, cumin and cocoa powder served with a quick side dish of black beans, grilled corn, cilantro and splash of lime juice. An quick and easy summer dinner, ready in under 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 1/2 tsp brown sugar
  • 1/2 tsp chipotle chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 lime, juice of
  • 4 6 oz Alaskan wild salmon fillets

Steps

  1. Combine all the spices in a small bowl, paprika to cocoa powder.
  2. Squeeze lime juice over salmon then rub in the spices to completely coat; set aside to rest about 5 minutes.
  3. Heat a clean outdoor grill (make sure the grates are clean and lightly oiled) or if cooking indoors a lightly oiled grill pan over high heat.
  4. When hot, grill the salmon covered for about 8 to 10 minutes or until the fish is opaque throughout.
  5. Serve with black beans, corn and lime wedges.
